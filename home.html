Welcome to Health Quotes! A Chatbot to Help Alleviate Depression!

To Questions To Quotes

Health Quotes is a two purpose Flask-Python-HTML powered chatbot website developed by Jatin Aggarwal, Abraham Kunnan, and Omar Khalid!

This website works as an easy to use resource for people struggling with depression!

This website has a text-trained chatbot that can answer many questions that you may have about depression.

In addition, this website also offers a chatbot that can give you inspiring quotes.

These quotes can either be general quotes or can be quotes related to the emotion detected in your input.

Click the hyperlinks to interact with the chatbot!
